About PPRA

The Public Procurement Regulatory Authority (PPRA) is dedicated to ensuring fair, efficient, and transparent procurement processes in the public sector. Our mission is to promote best practices in procurement and create a level playing field for all stakeholders.

Our Vision

To be the leading authority in public procurement regulation, fostering integrity and value for money in public spending.


Our Mission

To regulate, monitor, and promote best practices in public procurement for the achievement of national development goals.
